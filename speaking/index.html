Speaking

I’m a bit of an accidental public speaker. It turns out that one of the quickest ways to get over a fear of public speaking is to do something 1000x scarier at the same time.

Podcasts

The Local Club

Organising Tech in Sweden, October 2024

Sen Kanner, former chairperson of the Unionen club at Klarna and Henry Catalini Smith, former chairperson of the Unionen club at Spotify, are back to tell us all about what it takes to run a local union club. We look at what positions there are on the board, what the responsibilities of the chairperson are, how to get people engaged and involved, and how to be a leader without burning yourself out. Building a democratic workplace is hard (in fact, two kinds of hard), but for Sen and Henry, incredibly rewarding as well!

Organising Spotify

Organising Tech in Sweden, October 2024

Henry, founder of the Unionen club at Spotify, and Ralf, current vice chair of the club, tell the story of organising efforts at Spotify, educate us on organising strategies, and warn us against taking shortcuts. A CBA hasn't been won yet, but the saga of Spotify is far from over!
